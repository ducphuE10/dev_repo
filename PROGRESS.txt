## Codebase Patterns
(Add reusable patterns here as they are discovered during implementation)

- Feed queries must always filter `status = 'active'` — never return flagged/removed posts
- Affiliate click tracking happens server-side in the redirect endpoint, never in the frontend
- Always use `IF NOT EXISTS` for migration SQL statements
- Receipt images live in the private R2 bucket only — never expose receipt URLs to clients
- Background worker jobs (BullMQ) handle all heavy async work: video processing, OCR, counter updates
- Aggregation counters (upvote_count, flag_count) are updated via worker jobs, not inline in request handlers
